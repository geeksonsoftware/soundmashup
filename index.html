Sound Mashup. Share your sound

Sound Mashup let's you easily record any type of sound (voice, music, effects, etc) and combine them in creative and funny ways.

You can then share the audio creations to your friends via your favourite messaging app (WhatsApp, Threema, ...).

Easy to use

You can use the application by following four simple steps:

Record samples

Create a sequence

Combine recorded sound into a sequence

Share the result!

Privacy

All recorded audio never leave your device. We do not collect any audio samples from the application. The only way to share recorded audio is to share them explicitly via the app.

We do collect telemetry data and crash reports using Microsoft Application Insights in order to provide a better experience to our customers. We use Google AdMob to provide you interstitial ads, Google itself may or may not collect telemetry information to serve better suited ads.
